Logo & Iconography

Guidelines for using the STR Technologies logo and our system icons. Consistent use of these assets reinforces our brand identity and ensures visual harmony.

Main Logo

v1

This version is the original design

v2 - The "Soft Modern" Approach

Description: This is the most direct answer to the request. It takes the original block and applies a consistent radius to the corners.

Rationale: Rounded corners are friendlier and more modern than sharp 90-degree angles. This makes the brand feel more accessible and "digital-native" (like an app icon).

v3 - The "Corporate Gradient"

Description: Keeps the rounded shape but adds a subtle color gradient (fading from lighter to darker blue) and a slight gloss effect.

Rationale: The gradient adds depth and a "premium" feel. It stops the logo from looking flat and generic, giving it a more established, corporate presence without changing the geometry.

v4 - The "3D Tactile" Look

Description: Features a bevel/emboss effect on both the text and the container, making the letters look raised.

Rationale: This creates a sense of durability and substance. It works well for brands involved in hardware, construction, or manufacturing because it looks solid and "heavy."

v5 - The "Varsity/Framed" Style

Description: A rounded rectangle that features an internal white border line (stroke) framing the text.

Rationale: The internal frame draws the eye inward to the text and adds structure. It gives off a classic, slightly retro, or athletic vibe (reminiscent of automotive or sports team patches).

v6 - The "Social Badge"

Description: Adapts the logo into a circular format with an internal ring.

Rationale: Circles are the standard for social media avatars (Instagram, TikTok, etc.). This variation ensures the branding fits perfectly in those tight circular crops without cutting off corners. It feels like a stamp of approval.

Usage Rules

  • The logo should always be used in SVG format to ensure crisp rendering at any size.
  • Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "S" in STR.
  • Do not stretch, distort, or recolor the logo.
  • Use the white version on dark backgrounds and the primary blue version on light backgrounds.

Favicon

The favicon is used for browser tabs and bookmarks. It should be legible at small sizes (16x16px, 32x32px).
