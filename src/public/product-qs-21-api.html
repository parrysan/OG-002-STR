Product Overview

QS-21-Api is the major isomeric constituent (65%) of the QS-21 fraction derived from Quillaja saponaria bark, now available through advanced chemical synthesis. This saponin adjuvant has demonstrated exceptional ability to enhance both humoral and cell-mediated immune responses in preclinical and clinical vaccine trials.

Usage Guidelines

Reconstitution

  1. Allow vial to reach room temperature (15-25°C).
  2. Add sterile PBS (pH 7.4) to achieve target concentration.
  3. Gently swirl until dissolved (do not vortex).
  4. Use immediately or store at 2-8°C for < 24h.
