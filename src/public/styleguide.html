Writing Style & Tone

Our voice is Scientific, never Salesy. We prioritize precision, data, and reliability.

Do

  • "Synthetic QS-21-Api demonstrates equivalent activity."
  • "Guaranteed ≥98% purity verified by HPLC."
  • "Secure supply chain."

Don't

  • "Our amazing product boosts vaccines like never before."
  • "The best adjuvant on the market."
  • Using marketing jargon without data.

Avatars & Target Audience

These personas guide our UX priorities, content strategy, and design decisions — ensuring the site speaks credibly to the biotech research community.

Dr. Sarah Chen, PhD

Senior Vaccine Researcher
Boston, USA

Goal: Source high-purity, batch-consistent adjuvants with full documentation.

  • • Needs comprehensive regulatory docs (GMP)
  • • Frustrated by supply chain uncertainty
  • • Values scientific rigour and purity data

Michael Torres, MSc

Procurement Manager
Frankfurt, DE

Goal: Secure competitive bulk pricing and maintain audit-ready documentation.

  • • Needs downloadable COA / SDS
  • • Manages multiple supplier relationships
  • • Prioritizes reliable international shipping

Prof. James Okonkwo, PhD

Principal Investigator
London, UK

Goal: Access high-quality materials within grant budget constraints.

  • • Needs low minimum order quantities
  • • Focused on novel adjuvant combinations
  • • Values fast delivery and tech support
