Writing Style & Tone

Our voice is Scientific, never Salesy. We prioritize precision, data, and reliability.

Do

  • "Synthetic QS-21-Api demonstrates equivalent activity."
  • "Guaranteed ≥98% purity verified by HPLC."
  • "Secure supply chain."

Don't

  • "Our amazing product boosts vaccines like never before."
  • "The best adjuvant on the market."
  • Using marketing jargon without data.
