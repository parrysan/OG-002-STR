## 3. SEO & Connectivity

### 3.1 SEO Basics
- **Title Tags:** `Product Name | STR Technologies`
- **Meta Descriptions:** Unique for every page. Max 160 chars.
- **Alt Text:** All images must have descriptive alt text (e.g., "Chemical structure of QS-21-Api").

### 3.2 Analytics
- **Google Analytics 4 (GA4):** Setup and connected.
- **Google Search Console:** Property verified.

---

## 4. Enhancement: Performance Budget

**Goal:** Ensure the site feels "Premium" by being fast.

**Targets (Lighthouse / PageSpeed Insights):**
- **Performance:** > 90 (Mobile)
- **Accessibility:** 100
- **Best Practices:** 100
- **SEO:** 100

**Tactics:**
- **Image Optimization:** All JPEGs/PNGs compressed to WebP where possible.
- **Lazy Loading:** Enabled for below-the-fold images.
- **Script Management:** Defer non-critical JS.
